We make the stuff you won’t be able to travel without.

Our luggage is the perfect harmony between clever designs and premium materials.  Obsessed with comfort? So are we. That’s why we curated travel pillows, blankets and sets to keep you cozy, whether you’re braving a red-eye or the second shift driver on that road trip.

It’s important to be prepared for anything. We thought of these so that you don’t have to, and we just know you’ll thank us later for them. Adapters, converters, luggage locks… don’t sweat the small stuff.

You are one in a million, and your luggage should be, too! Express yourself and stand out from the pack with our thoughtfully curated luggage tags and passport holders. Planning a beach trip? We have what you never realized you need: hammocks, beach chair towel clips, drink holders and more.
